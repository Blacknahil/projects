Welcome to Tutor Linkup

What Tutor Linkup is ?

Tutor linkup is a platform designed to connect college students, who can offer tutoring and mentorship,with families seeking such services.

What We Do?

Connect Tutors with Students that match their Schedule and Location.

Connect Tutors with Parents spending little time guiding their kids

Connect Mentors who want to guide Younger Generations

Connect Students with opportunities to give back to their community by helping people in need.

How Tutor Linkup Works?

1. Create An Account

2. Edit Profile

3. Post What You Are Looking For.

4. Make Use of Our Searching and Recommendation Feature

5. Connect and Chat

6. Start Your Job as A Tutor or Start Learning as A Student.
